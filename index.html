Banana Smoothie

Bananas are a great choice for making homemade smoothies. Use them as a base and add different fruits for a quick and easy healthy snack.

Ingredients

  • Banana
  • Yogurt
  • Ice
  • Choose your own fruit juice
  • Choose your own fruit

Directions

  1. Remove seeds and peels
  2. Place all items in a blender
  3. Put top on blender and blend until smooth
  4. Drink & Enjoy :)
